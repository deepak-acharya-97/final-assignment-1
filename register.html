Instructions to fill online application form

  1. Fill the below application form.
  2. Please upload the scanned copy of relevant documents mentioned.
  3. Please mention your mobile number and email id properly as we will be creating groups with the help of those.
  4. Please mention your address and landmark properly. It will be helpful during house visit.
  5. We will be updating the acknowledgement for the application reviewed in your personal page.

Instructions to fill offline application form

  1. Fill the copy of application form sent to your school/from anywhere.
  2. Attach the following documents.
    1. Aadhar Card
    2. SSLC marks card
    3. Ration Card
    4. Fee paid certificate to college(if available)
  3. Post us the application form and relevant documents self attested.

General guidelines to be followed

  1. Personally you have to be present during scholarship distribution. Others aren't allowed.
  2. You can come with your parents but parents can't recieve the scholarship amount given to you.
  3. You will be mapped to a particular donor.
  4. You have to write 4 letters in a year to continue with Vidyaposhak.
  5. You should get minimum 70%(CGPA 7) in order to renewal the application.
